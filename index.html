About The Mbulaheni Group

Mbulaheni Group is a premier black-owned holding company based in Pretoria, South Africa. Established in 2015, our diversified investment portfolio spans multiple essential service sectors.

Our divisions operate across five core sectors: civil engineering & construction, IT services, fuel supply, commercial cleaning, and security services.
