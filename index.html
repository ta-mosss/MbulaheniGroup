About The Mbulaheni Group

Mbulaheni Group is a premier black-owned holding company based in Pretoria, South Africa. Established in 2015, our diversified investment portfolio spans civil engineering, IT, fuel, cleaning, and security sectors.

We combine international standards with local expertise to deliver quality solutions to businesses, government entities, and households across South Africa.

We are committed to transforming communities through job creation, skills development, and sustainable business practices.
