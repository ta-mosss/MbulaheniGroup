About The Mbulaheni Group

Mbulaheni Group is a premier black-owned holding company based in Pretoria, South Africa. Established in 2015, our diversified investment portfolio spans multiple essential service sectors with a focus on innovation and playing a vital role in strengthening South Africa's economic growth and development.

Our divisions operate across five core sectors: civil engineering and construction, information technology services, fuel supply, commercial cleaning, and security services. We combine international standards with local expertise to deliver quality solutions to businesses, government entities, and households across the African continent.

At Mbulaheni Group, we are committed to transforming communities through job creation, skills development, and sustainable business practices that make a meaningful difference in people's lives.
